The Farr Commons aims to bring together investigators across the Farr Institute so that they can easily and securely, share and reuse methodology and data.

The Farr Commons serves as a pilot for the NIH Research Object Commons - “a conceptual framework for a digital environment to allow efficient storage, manipulation, and sharing of research objects”.

FarrInstitute: http://www.farinstitute.org
NIH Research Object Commons: http://bd2k.nih.gov/commons.html
